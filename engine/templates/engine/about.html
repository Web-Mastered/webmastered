About Engine

Overview

Engine is a fast and lightweight CMS built by Web Mastered Ltd. It's based on the open-source Wagtail CMS project, additional features, tools and refinements have been added, allowing a feature-rich website and blog to be created. The websites and web applications that we at Web Mastered Ltd deliver, use Engine as a starting point, we add extra functionalities and features to it, to deliver a stunning and powerful final product for our clients. We make sure that the products we develop wrap around the client's company rather than the other way round.

Speed and efficiency are a few of the main objectives of Engine, the engineers at Web Mastered Ltd hand-craft each line of code to be as fast and efficient as possible. This allows Engine to power even the biggest websites, handling thousands of visitors and hosting thousands of pages effortlessly.

Upgrading

Engine websites are built to be as efficient as possible with beskpoke and lightweight code, but as your website grows and your your website's visitor count and page count starts to increase, you may notice that your website may start to run slower. You can get around this issue by upgrading to a more powerful server subscription. Simply contact us through our website, www.web-mastered.com, and our engineers will move you to a more powerful server.

You can track your Engine website's performance via the Metrics page in Engine admin settings, click here to visit that page. If you find your hardware utilisation is high and your website is slow, it might be beneficial to upgrade to a more powerful server to keep up with your growing website.

Support

Engine is engineered to be as low maintenance as possible. Engine is built to run 24-hours everyday, year-in and year-out, non-stop. It is a very rare occurance if an Engine powered website starts to not work as intended, if this happens, please contact us through our website, www.web-mastered.com, and our engineers will get your website back up and running as soon as possible.

Unlike our content management system (CMS) counterparts, such as WordPress, Engine and its dependencies don't have to be updated regularly. It is a very rare occurance to have a security vulnerability. As we host our clients' Engine website, we will carry out regular maintanance, ensuring your website runs as smooth as possible, we will also make sure to upgrade outdated softwares and patch any high-risk vulnerabilites that may affect your website.
