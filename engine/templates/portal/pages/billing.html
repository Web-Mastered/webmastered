Billing

We use a third-party payment gateway provider, called Stripe, to handle payments in the safest and most efficient way possible. To manage your billing information, click the "Open Billing Portal" button below. From the billing portal, you can change your payment method and billing information and you can view your billing history and hosting plan.

Quick Note

Please note that details which are used for your billing account, such as email, can be different to that used for your WM account. Any notifications regarding billing will be sent to your billing email address, and any notifications regarding your WM service will be sent to the email address marked as Primary in the WM Client Portal.

Feel free to contact us if you have any queries.
